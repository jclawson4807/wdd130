Try one of our popular Whitewater Rafting Trips!

Blossom Bar

$125/person

This single day trip includes 20 miles of medium difficulty river rafting, along with a wonderful lunch cooked over cedar wood charcoal. Great for teams, inclding families with teenage children.

Clavey Falls

$210/person

This two day trip includes 47 miles of low difficulty river rafting along a tree lined river with three distinct types of rapids, fishing, and two nights of camping. Great for large groups, inclding families with young children. All meals and sleeping facilities provided.

Green Wall

$400/person

This thrilling three day trip includes over 75 miles of high difficulty river rafting. Navigate four sets of rapids and a waterfall. Participants can enjoy fly or lure fishing, four nights of camping, and a hiking tour of a beautiful mountain range. All meals and sleeping facilities provided.
